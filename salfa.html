It is not just a board game, but while playing it with your friends, you will feel a lot of excitement and fun.

“Salfa” begins with a hint told by the storyteller, who changes with the beginning of each round. The hint is inspired by one of the picture cards in the storyteller’s hands, while the rest of the players in each round try to reveal his card by guessing the right card, and at the same time the rest of the players try to choose a card that resembles The storyteller’s hint. At the end of each round, each player moves steps based on his/her choices.

“Salfa” is different everytime you play, containing 180 images specially drawn for the game by artists, including Kuwaitis, and this large number of images opens the way for an endless number of stories and hints, in addition to 45 action cards that will increase the intensity of competition and twists that take place in each round.

In the end, “Salfa” is not a traditional board game, as it is an ideal meeting place to retrieve the best stories and memories that are the basis and foundation of the game, and rest assured, every game will be unique and different.
