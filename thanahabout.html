About US

Thannah Factories, a specializing in the preparation and manufacture of blonde and green Saudi coffee in innovative ways through capsules of various use, using the best manufacturing and packaging practices.

Our factory relies on the best applications in manufacturing capsules that are compatible with all international machines and presenting them through various packaging and boxes for personal or commercial use.

A real Saudi coffee experience.

Natural Ingredients

Our coffee and our beans are from direct primary sources, whether Harari coffee or Khawlani coffee, and we prepare them accurately by our laboratories and ensure the stability of our quality.

To ensure quality and accuracy, we test every batch of our produce through independent laboratories to ensure that every cup is rich in taste and full of authenticity.

Exquisite Flavour

Thannah coffee capsules are carefully roasted and prepared in our own ways, to offer the famous flavors of Saudi complicated coffee.

Local and Ethical

We believe in sustainability, health, environment, workers and society. Therefore, we are keen that our raw materials are obtained ethically and legally to contribute to the vision of our Saudi country on prosperity and sustainability.

Our long-term sustainability strategy includes increasing the internal content of our products to nearly 100% recyclable.
