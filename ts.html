\r\n Article 3: Vehicle Usage\r\n

\r\n

\r\n The lessee undertakes that the leased vehicle will not be used for:\r\n

\r\n
    \r\n
  • \r\n Any purpose other than that for which the leased vehicle has been designed,\r\n including not exceeding its designed load capacity.\r\n
    • \r\n
  • \r\n Racing or any other kind of competitive sport exceeding the speeding limit in\r\n force etc.\r\n
    • \r\n
  • \r\n Carrying passengers, animals or goods other than frozen and chilled products\r\n for hire.\r\n
    • \r\n
  • \r\n The transportation of any hazardous, explosive of inflammable material or any\r\n goods or items those are likely to damage the vehicle – interior, exterior or mechanical.\r\n
    • \r\n
  • Transportation of illegal substances.\r\n
    • \r\n
  • Any activities which will render the lessor’s insurance cover void or result\r\n in the vehicle being impounded by the authorities.\r\n
    • \r\n
