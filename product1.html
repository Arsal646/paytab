STORM KIMONOS® proudly introduces the Stealth Matrix MX3 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Kimono. The Stealth Matrix MX3 jacket is constructed of an extremely comfortable 420gsm Storm proprietary Japanese Weave Fabric, and with Featherlight® STORM proprietary Breathe Tech Fabric Tailored Pants.

Manufactured with Preshrunk fabric and ultra-resistant, breathable materials, the Stealth Matrix MX3 features a Zero Warp Collar, Zero Hold Technology®, a hidden mouth-guard pocket, 3D high-definition embroidery, distinctive "STORM Red" inside detailing and subtle contrast stitching, as well as an Anti-Microbial Treatment, and is designed to comply with the most updated IBJJF regulations.
